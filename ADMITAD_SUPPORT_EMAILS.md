## Quick Tips:

- **Pick the one** that sounds most like you
- **Add your name** and website URL
- **Keep it short** - they're busy, so brief is better
- **Be friendly** but professional
- **Follow up** in a week if you don't hear back

## Where to Send:

- Check your Admitad dashboard for "Support" or "Contact" button
- Or look for a support email address in your account settings
